Ready to make some music?!?

(Make sure Sonic Pi is open and your new song will play!)

Not particularly musically talented? Still want to create amazing harmonic pieces of musical art? This online web page was created so anyone with Internet access can generate beautiful music. All you have to do is download and open up the app “Sonic Pi,” pick a song that you like, and hit Play! A new song based off the song you picked will be generated and will play automatically by Markov Chain analysis and a touch of Music Theory.
